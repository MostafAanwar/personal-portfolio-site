About this project

This is a prototype website was made part of crisis Hackx MENA hackathon to help against Covid-19. In the current days we are living in corona-virus known as COVID-19 has made us change our daily routine drastically which caused a lot of changes whether do we work from home or we have to go down and work from the office and a lot of other aspects of our lives has changed. Most of the countries are under complete or partial lock-down or a curfew, for example in Egypt the lock-down starts from the night so people are only allowed to go in the morning mostly for essential needs such as getting food supplies or medicines. The main problem here is if people are not cautious enough when going around (if not keeping social distance and other precautions) so in our efforts to try and minimize that and reduce people gatherings as much as possible in order to reduce the spread of the virus we made a website that would help people to search in categories ( supermarkets, pharmacies ...etc ) in the areas around them depending on the crowdedness of the place from the intended category. In addition to that, you can also search by how far/close is a needed place in order for us to help you go to a less crowded shop or reschedule your shopping.
